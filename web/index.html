Why teams use it

Glasspane shows chosen payouts, recovered amounts, memos, and tamper results without handing reviewers a wallet-wide viewing key.

Local verification

Room reports are generated by the Rust verifier and reproducible locally. Single receipts can also be verified in-browser from receipt JSON and raw transaction hex on the create page.
