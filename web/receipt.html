How Glasspane works

Every shielded Zcash output is encrypted twice, once to the receiver and once to sender context.

A receipt discloses one specific OCK. Anyone holding it can decrypt exactly that one output and learn recipient, value, and memo. Other outputs remain opaque because each output has a different OCK.

The cryptography is the same primitive already in the Zcash protocol and tested against published vectors for Orchard and Sapling.
